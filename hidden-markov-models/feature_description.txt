Feature variations used in the notebook

short: rolling windows of 5, 10, and 20 trading days
medium: rolling window of 60 trading days
long: rolling windows of 120 and 252 trading days
spot: current-day or non-rolling version
anchor252: spread versus the 252-day moving average anchor
robust: rolling median/IQR scaled version of the same feature

Feature descriptions

returns_mean (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling average of daily log returns, capturing the local drift of the market over each horizon.

returns_cumulative (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling sum of daily log returns, measuring total log performance over the window.

drawdown_current (short, medium, long, robust)
Current log-price drawdown from the rolling peak, showing how far the market sits below its recent high.

drawdown_max (short, medium, long, robust)
Worst drawdown observed within the rolling window, measuring peak-to-trough stress severity.

volatility_std (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling standard deviation of daily log returns, used as realized volatility.

volatility_variance (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling variance of daily log returns, emphasizing high-volatility episodes more strongly than standard deviation.

volatility_of_vol (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling standard deviation of realized volatility, capturing instability in the volatility process itself.

volatility_sqret_autocorr1 (short, medium, long, robust)
Lag-1 autocorrelation of squared returns, measuring volatility clustering and persistence.

volatility_absret_mean (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling mean of absolute returns, a robust proxy for market turbulence.

vix_level (spot, robust)
Current VIX level, representing contemporaneous implied market fear.

vix_returns (spot, robust)
Daily log return of the VIX, capturing changes in implied volatility expectations.

vix_mean (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling average of the VIX level, summarizing sustained implied-volatility conditions.

vix_std (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling standard deviation of the VIX level, measuring instability in implied volatility.

vix_to_realizedvol (short, medium, long, robust)
Ratio of VIX level to realized volatility, comparing implied risk pricing with recent realized risk.

distribution_skew (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling skewness of returns, describing asymmetry between upside and downside moves.

distribution_kurtosis (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling kurtosis of returns, measuring tail thickness and the prevalence of extreme moves.

distribution_downside_semivariance (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling mean squared negative returns, isolating downside volatility.

distribution_upside_semivariance (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling mean squared positive returns, isolating upside volatility.

distribution_tail_ratio (short, medium, long, robust)
Ratio of the rolling 95th percentile return to the rolling 5th percentile return, summarizing tail balance and asymmetry.

momentum_cumulative (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling cumulative log return used as a momentum signal over each horizon.

trend_slope (short, medium, long, robust)
Linear regression slope of log price within the window, capturing directional trend strength.

sharpe_rolling (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling mean return divided by rolling return volatility, summarizing risk-adjusted trend quality.

price_to_ma (short, medium, long, robust)
Distance between price and its rolling moving average, indicating overextension or weakness relative to trend.

ma_spread (short, medium, anchor252, robust)
Difference between a shorter moving average and the 252-day moving average, measuring short-versus-long trend separation.

liquidity_volume_mean (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling average trading volume, representing background market activity and depth.

liquidity_volume_shock (short, medium, long, robust)
Ratio of current volume to its rolling mean, highlighting unusual trading surges or droughts.

liquidity_amihud (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling average of the Amihud illiquidity measure, defined from absolute return per unit of volume.

liquidity_absret_to_volume (short, medium, long, robust)
Ratio of cumulative absolute return to cumulative volume, capturing price impact relative to trading activity.

breadth_positive_days_pct (short, medium, long, robust)
Fraction of days with positive returns inside the window, acting as a market breadth proxy from the index itself.

breadth_up_down_ratio (short, medium, long, robust)
Ratio of up days to down days in the window, measuring directional participation balance.

tail_var (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling 5 percent Value at Risk from historical returns, estimating typical downside threshold under stress.

tail_expected_shortfall (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling average return beyond the 5 percent VaR cutoff, measuring expected loss in the tail.

tail_max_abs_return (short, medium, long, robust)
Largest absolute daily return within the window, capturing the strongest shock realized recently.

tail_extreme_count (short, medium, long, robust)
Count of days where absolute return exceeds two times rolling volatility, measuring the frequency of outsized moves.

correlation_return_vix (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling correlation between equity returns and VIX returns, reflecting the return-risk sentiment linkage.

correlation_return_vol (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling correlation between returns and realized volatility, describing how performance co-moves with market turbulence.

correlation_leverage_effect (short, medium, long, robust)
Rolling correlation between returns and changes in realized volatility, capturing the leverage-effect style downside-volatility relationship.

shock_absret_gt_2p5std (short, medium, long, robust)
Binary indicator for days when absolute return exceeds 2.5 times rolling volatility, flagging abrupt market shocks.

shock_vol_spike (short, medium, long, robust)
Binary indicator for days when realized volatility exceeds its rolling 90th percentile, flagging volatility spike regimes.
